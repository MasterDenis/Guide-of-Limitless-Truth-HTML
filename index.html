Shadow work is a practice of facing the darker and rejected parts of ourselves, in purpise of integrating them.
Everyone has a shadow self, and realizing that is the first step to self discovery.

The goal of a shadow work session is to identify the parts of you that have been living in a "shadow", and put you on a right direction toward exploring and healing that part of you.
Shadow work is a crusial part of both self development and spirituality. It can help you gain insight about many areas or struggles in your life, but mostly how it relates to you as a person, on a deep level.

It's the best tool for boosting your self discovery journey and an opportunity for awaknining while in hard or confusing circumstances. Everythign in our life is a reflection of the relationship you have with yourself,
thats why life consists of 10% of what happens to you, and 90% of how you react to it - internally or externally.

My sessions are based on energy & card readings, but unlike classic card and tarot readings, my practice has nothing to do with fortune tellings or predictions.
It's an alternative use, in purpose of utilizing this tool as a self discovery instrument. With years of practice in areas of intuitive and energetical work, you are sure to be in good hands.

Light work involves empowering yourself through living in alignment with your higher self, which is the most connected and potent part of yourself. It can be done through focusing on energy, practice, habits or characteristics that bring the most positive influence into your inner or outer world.
