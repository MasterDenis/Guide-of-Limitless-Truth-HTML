Shadow & Light are inseparable aspects of human psyche and spirit, and both are equally important for a healthy and balanced personal self development and spirituality.

Shadow work consists of working on the aspects of yourself or your life, that are usually avoided, resisted or rejected. Shadow work is an extremely important practice as it often collides with our light work, and blocks our ability to perceive, experience or work on positive emotions or aspects of ourselves.

Light work involves empowering yourself through living in alignment with your higher self, which is the most connected and potent part of yourself. It can be done through focusing on energy, practice, habits or characteristics that bring the most positive influence into your inner or outer world.
