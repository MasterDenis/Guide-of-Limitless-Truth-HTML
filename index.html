Few Things about me

My name is Ewa, and in media I am know as Guide of Limitless Truth.

My passion for spirituality started in 2017 and since then I dived into a lot of themes and practices related to metaphysics, esoterism, parapsychology and self development.
This came out to be my life passion, and along with it I discovered tarot, which I came out to be more passionate about than I initially would've expected.

Through-out my journey I created a spiritually themed YouTube channel which now has a count of almost 10k subscribers and some videos with over 200k views. Currently I am also a streamer on twitch and I happily make free of charge readings for people, which lead me to realize how much fulfillment I find in reading for others.

I am also an artist from a very young age, and I specialize in both traditional and digital art. Additionally I am also a huge music lover.

Services

All of us go through life trying to discover who we are, yet many of us mistake unhealed and hurt parts of ourselves for who we are and what our life is supposed to look like. We try different things to have a better and happier life, yet we forget that happiness is unconditional. Instead of chasing unattainable happiness, we should rather thrive to discover what about us, blocks us from self alignment.

My purpose is to provide people with guidance in order to bringing an individual into more authentic and self aware direction in their life and mindset. The key to that is to make one self aware enough to set them up for transforming themselves and their experience of life.

In my sessions I choose a fully individual approach to each and every person I come in contact with, since all of us are different, and we need different things, at different times.
My sessions are focused on directly addressing all the blockages, limitations as well as mental and emotional struggles, that may be blocking you from freely navigating through your life. Think of it as being in a warm, genuine hug with all the dark, rejected and ignored fragments of yourself . . .

My sessions are based on Energy reading, which means channeling your personal field of energy, thoughts and emotions.

You could compare it to empathy, but on way more advanced level.
I will be indirectly feeling your emotions, and understanding your mindset on life, thus giving you sense of self awareness.
My main channeling tools are Tarot & Oracle Cards, and that's what my sessions are based on.
I will be using my expertise in spirituality, self development, psyche, as well as my personal philosophy and life experience to guide you through the session and help You build a better approach to life.

Disclaimers:  I do not provide financial, medical or legal advices through my sessions. 
THIS IS NOT FORTUNE TELLING.
